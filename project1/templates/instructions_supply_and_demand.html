Background Gameplay Quarterly Reports Supply & Demand Review & Restock

There are 3 sets of data which are related to and/or affect demand and data directly. They are:

Demand Forecasts Are unit demand estimates for each product broken into quarters. They are provided at the start of each year and may not be completely representative of demand conditions due to unforeseen events and regular variability of demand - which tends to fall within a 5% margin of error on a daily basis.

Supplier Quotes Are provided to you by suppliers on an annual basis. The quotations provide visibility over each products'

  • Selling price
  • Unit cost
  • Delivery truckload (setup) cost
  • Annual per-unit inventory holding cost
  • Delivery lead time
  • Max truckload unit limit

    • News & Events Are random and unforeseen occurrences that may affect either demand or supply conditions, and are not accounted for by demand forecasts and supplier quotations at the start of each year. Luckily, your team of experienced operations specialists tend to provide a projected impact summary whenever such events occur.
