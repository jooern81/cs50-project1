Background Gameplay Quarterly Reports Supply & Demand Review & Restock

The Quarterly reports tab provides you with information reflecting past performance. They are broken into quarters & consists of the following datasets:

Profitability Report - quarterly bottom line numbers which include details on:

  • Revenue From Sales - actual revenue generated from sales in each quarter.
  • Restock Setup Expense - actual expenses for setting up restocks.
  • Restock Unit Expense - actual expenses for purchasing inventory.
  • Inventory Holding Costs - actual expenses on inventory holding.
  • Operational P&L quarterly profitability.


    • Sales Report - a table summary of sales performance for the respective quarter with values of:

  • Unit Sales - reflects the unit count of each product sold for the respective quarter.
  • Estimated Missed Sales - reflects forecasted (but not necessarily actual) demand which was not met over the quarter due to situations of stockout.


    • Inventory Report - overview of inventory holdings per quarter, split by:

  • Starting Inventory - inventory in stock for each product at quarters' start.
  • Ending Inventory - inventory in stock for each product at quarters' end.


    • *Operational P&L values of other players may be viewed during play-through but other datasets may only be viewed at the end of the game. Be sure to review how others managed their Supermarkets at the end of the game to improve your skills in Operations Management.
