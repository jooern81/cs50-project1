Background Gameplay Supply & Demand Panels Review & Restock Panel

You (or your group) are the new Procurement Manager of one of many supermarket chains in the city of Bufferville. As the new manager in charge, you are tasked to make periodic but timely restocking decisions for the various products your supermarket carries. Thanks to systems your predecessor had previously set up, a number of collaterals are prepared by the procurement team to aid your decision process.

These include:

  • Annual demand forecasts modelled at the start of each fiscal year.
  • Supplier quotations which are made regularly and include respective holding costs.
  • News & events shortlist which summarises all news and events flagged as relevant and important to either product supply or demand by your colleagues.
  • Inventory Data found under review and restock, which is generated in real time thanks to your supermarket's proprietary tracking system.
  • Quarterly Reports compiled on past performance, inventory levels, profitability, etc.


    • You may learn more about each of these report types in their respective tabs.

    The above data, paired with Operations Management methodologies, will be critical for optimising the profitability of your supermarket. You should remember that bad stocking decisions can lead to stockouts and missed sales. At the end of your tenure, your the profitability performance of your supermarket will be ranked against that of other chains in the district. Accordingly, rewards and consequences may follow.

    Good luck, Manager
